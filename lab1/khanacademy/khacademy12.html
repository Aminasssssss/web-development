Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 min
  • Number of people: 4
Ingredients Quantity
onion 2
cucumber 3

Step 1: Heat and Assemble: Place one slice of bread, butter-side down, in a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat, then layer both slices of cheese on top.

Step 2: Cook and Flip:

  1. top with the second slice of bread
  2. cook for 3 to 4 minutes until the bottom is golden brown
  3. then flip and grill the other side until the cheese is fully melted
